## 练习 2.18 请定义出过程 reverse，它以一个表为参数，返回的表中所包含的元素与参数表相同，但排列顺序与参数表相反：

```
(reverse (list 1 4 9 16 25))
(25 16 9 4 1)
```

---

```eval-scheme
(define (append list1 list2)
  (if (null? list1)
    list2
    (cons (car list1) (append (cdr list1) list2)))
)

(define (reverse lst) 
  (if (null? (cdr lst))
      lst
      (append (reverse (cdr lst)) (list (car lst)))))

(reverse (list 1 4 9 16 25))
```
