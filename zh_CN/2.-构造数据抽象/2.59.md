## 练习 2.59 请为采用未排序表的集合实现定义union-set操作。

---

```eval-scheme
(define (element-of-set? x set)
    (cond ((null? set) false)
        ((equal? x (car set)) #t)
        (else (element-of-set? x (cdr set)))
    )
)

(element-of-set? 1 (list 1 2 3 4))
```

```eval-scheme
(define union-set append)

(union-set (list 1 2 3 4) (list 5 6 7 8))
```
